Harar

Harar is a city located in eastern Ethiopia, known for its rich history and cultural significance. It was once a major trading center and is now known for its beautiful architecture, including the Harar Jugol, the old walled city, which was declared a World Heritage site by UNESCO in 2006. Harar is also home to a vibrant arts scene and is known for its coffee, which is considered some of the best in the world

Important Phone numbers in Ethiopia
939 for fire
907 for ambulance service
991 for police
911 for the national emergency number
How To Get To Harar

To get to Harar, you can travel by car, bus, or plane. The city is located about 525 kilometers east of Addis Ababa and can be reached by driving, taking a bus, or flying to the nearest airport in Dire Dawa and then taking a taxi or hiring a car. It is advisable to plan your trip in advance and book your transportation accordingly.
